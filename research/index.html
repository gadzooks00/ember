Research

The EMBER Lab is proud to contribute to the broader scientific community through a steady output of high-impact publications. Our research has been featured in leading journals, showcasing advances in brain-computer interfaces, neurosurgical methods, and electrophysiological techniques. Each publication reflects the collaborative nature of our lab, drawing from the expertise of clinicians, engineers, and neuroscientists alike. Here, you can explore our peer-reviewed papers, conference proceedings, and other key publications that highlight our contributions to the field.
